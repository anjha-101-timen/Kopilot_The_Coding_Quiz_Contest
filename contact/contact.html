Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report an issue with an exam or contest?
Use the form above and pick “Exam / Quiz Issue” or “Contest Issue”. Share the quiz/contest name and what went wrong.
My CP / rank looks wrong—what should I send?
Select “CP / Level / Rank” and include the page you were on and what changed. A screenshot helps.
Can I request new quizzes, topics, or contests?
Yes—choose “Feature Request” and tell us the topic, difficulty, and what you want to practice.
