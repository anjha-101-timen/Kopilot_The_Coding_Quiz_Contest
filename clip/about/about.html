Our Story

Coding Quiz Contest began with a simple goal: create a single place where learners can practice coding concepts, participate in contests, and measure progress with clear scoring and CP rewards. Instead of jumping between scattered resources, you get exams, contests, results, and leaderboard in one flow.

Our focus is clarity and consistency—structured quizzes, smooth navigation, and a gamified progress system that keeps you coming back to improve every day.
